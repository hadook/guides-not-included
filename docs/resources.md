# Resources

---

## :lucide-drill: Tools

### [Wiki](https://oxygennotincluded.wiki.gg/)
The wiki has information about dupes, resources, critters, geysers, asteroids, and pretty much everything else you can find in the game.

### [ONI Database](https://oni-db.com/)
Provides a quick reference guide on game objects and their properties.

### [Professor Oakshell's Lab](https://www.professoroakshell.com/)
Includes a bunch of useful calculators, including food, farming, heat and rockets.

### [Blueprint Not Included](https://blueprintnotincluded.org/)
A site for creating and sharing blueprints.

---

## :lucide-messages-square: Social

### [Klei forums](https://forums.kleientertainment.com)
On Klei's forums you can read about updates to the game, learn about mods, submit a suggestion or a bug report, and more.

### [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/Oxygennotincluded)
Reddit's Oxygen Not Included subforum is a good place to talk about the game, see builds, or ask questions about your own.

### [Discord](https://discord.gg/EBncbX2)
The Oxygen Not Included discord has announcements, information about game updates, discussions and more.

---

## :lucide-book-open: Guides

### [Steam guides](https://steamcommunity.com/app/457140/guides/)
There are lots of great guides on Steam, written by gamers and covering pretty much anything you might want to learn. A special mention goes to the [Compendium of Amazing Designs](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2154398396) by Kharnath.
