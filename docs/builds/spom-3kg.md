# SPOM, Oxygen: 3kg/s (Full Rodriguez)


## Description

This SPOM is the so-called "Full Rodriguez". It generates 2.975 kg/second of oxygen.


!!! abstract "Construction"

    **Atmo sensor, upper (hydrogen)**: above 250  
    **Atmo sensors, lower (oxygen)**: above 450

    Use gold (or better) for things that can overheat. The upper section can be fully enclosed, as the hydrogen cools the top level. What material to build the liquid pipes out of depends on the temperature of your incoming water. If the incoming water is colder than 70°C you can use the water to cool down the surrounding area by using radiant pipes.
