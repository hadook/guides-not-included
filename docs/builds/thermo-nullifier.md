# Anti Entropy Thermo-Nullifier cooling


## Description

This design uses a conductive door controlled by a thermo sensor to keep a body of liquid at the desired temperature. It works well for instance with a SPOM, feeding in oxygen for cooling and excess hydrogen from the SPOM to power the cooling loop.

!!! info

    **Thermo sensor**: below [temperature you want coolant to be].  
    Use steel (or better conducting material) for the door.

    Powering the door is optional. This design has an overflow gas piping that feeds the anti entropy thermo-nullifier first and only fills the room when there is extra hydrogen.

!!! tip

    You can further improve the temperature transfer by adding tempshift plates so they touch all three metal tiles. (Thanks to several people on [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/Oxygennotincluded/comments/sxsun6/understanding_heat_transfer_mechanics_spom_cooling/) for that tip. See the comments for their additional ideas on improving heat transfer.)
